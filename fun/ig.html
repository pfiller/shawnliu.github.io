My friend Barry and I made a little thing called doevery.day. I want a calendar to track my drawing each day, so that I can see the streak I'm building––and have that as a motivation to keep going (so I don't break the chain). As of today I'm on a 32-day streak!

Just as important as the streak, I want my calendar to be public so my friends can see my progress and encourage me. Or maybe just knowing that someone might see my doevery.day page pushes me forward?
